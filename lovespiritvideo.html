After Pride Video as Branding Advertisign Campagin for Love Spirit Vodka

About the Brand

Love Spirit is a premium Vodka, produced using modern recipes and techniques, while still maintaining a traditional Spirit. The distillation process produces the purest ingredients resulting in a mild and round Vodka taste that can be taken neat or, if you prefer, with a little ice. Love Spirit’s rich aroma and mild taste are created by sourcing only the purest water and wheat from Swedish farms. Swedish quality through and through.

About the Challenge

Ready to be the sponsor of the Benidorm Pride 2019, Love Spirit asked our team to squeeze the event and obtain the best of it like they do in their insdustry. They needed to start shining, to get brand awareness and to start positioning as a LGBTQ+ supporter brand without forgeting to be a brand for everyone who believes in having fun. Thus, we got some keypoints turned into connotations inside the video. From them, the ones standing out most are: the inclusivity with all the LGBTQ+ communities, the Love Spirit and the fun.

Art Direction

From the fotography direction we set a close and fun tone which involves audience as part of the event. We wanted eyes and smiles to catch everyone watching the video, we wanted them trespassing the screen and coming inside to live what is going on. We seeked a colourful scenery where viewer can see what we saw from the inside and feel its energy and joy. As a piece of branding, we needed detailed shots were product is highlighted and to provide the attributes of Love Spirit we tried to be as inclusive as we could. As a result of this, we have a carefully treated video where photography direction is key with many close-up and dynamic shots.
